COFFEE


Coffee is an excellent beverage that provides an instant boost of energy. It contains caffeine in it that provides an instant feel of awakeness.


Different coffee drinks

  • americano
  • iced americano
  • expresso
  • latte
  • iced latte
  • cappucino
  • dalgona

Recipes

  • Americano
    1. Crush coffee beans.
    2. Brew an expresso shot.
    3. In a glass, add the expresso shot.
    4. Add hot water.

  • Iced Americano
    1. Crush coffee beans.
    2. Brew an expresso shot.
    3. In a glass add ice cubes.
    4. Add the expresso shot.
    5. Add cold water.

  • Latte
    1. Crush coffee beans.
    2. Brew an expresso.
    3. Steam and froth milk.
    4. Add the expresso to a teacup
    5. Pour inthe steamed milk.
    6. Add some sugar to taste.
    7. Enjoy!
